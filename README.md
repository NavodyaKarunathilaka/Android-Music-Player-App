# 🎵 MusicHub - Android Music Player

MusicHub is a modern Android music streaming application developed in **Kotlin** using **Android Studio**. It features a clean and intuitive UI to deliver a seamless user experience for music lovers.

---

## 🎯 **Overview**

MusicHub offers:

- 🔒 **User Authentication:** Secure Sign In and Sign Up using email and password
- 🎶 **Music Discovery:** Explore recently played tracks and popular radio stations
- ❤️ **Favorites System:** Save and shuffle your favorite songs
- 📂 **Playlist Management:** Create, manage, and play custom playlists
- ▶️ **Song Playback:** Play songs with queue support and smooth navigation
- 👤 **User Profile:** View account details and manage downloads

---

## ✨ **Key Features**

✔ **Secure Login/Signup**  
 – Email & password authentication

✔ **Personalized Home**  
 – Recently played tracks & popular radio stations

✔ **Favorites System**  
 – Save, view, and shuffle favorite songs

✔ **Playlist Support**  
 – Create and manage playlists

✔ **Seamless Music Playback**  
 – Song play, pause, skip, and queue features

✔ **User Profile**  
 – View account details 

---

## 🛠 **Tech Stack**

- **Language:** Kotlin
- **IDE:** Android Studio
